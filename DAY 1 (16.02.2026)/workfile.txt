General Instructions
1. Follow PEP 8 standards strictly.
2. Every function must contain a proper docstring.
3. Validate all user inputs.
4. Raise appropriate exceptions (TypeError, ValueError) where necessary.
5. Use modular design (functions for each logical unit).
6. Use type(), id(), dir(), and help() wherever applicable.
7. Avoid using advanced concepts not covered (no classes, no lists/dictionaries unless
required in capstone).
